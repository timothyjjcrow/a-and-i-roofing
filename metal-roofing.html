Metal Roofing

Metal roofing is one of the most durable and long-lasting roofing options available today. With a lifespan of 40-70 years, metal roofs provide exceptional protection against harsh weather conditions, including heavy rain, snow, high winds, and fire.

At A&I Roofing, we offer premium metal roofing systems that combine durability with aesthetic appeal. Our expert team specializes in the installation of various metal roofing styles that can enhance the appearance of your home or commercial building while providing superior protection for decades.

Exceptional longevity (40-70 years)
Superior weather resistance
Energy efficient & eco-friendly
Virtually maintenance-free
Fire resistant (Class A rating)
Wide variety of styles and colors

Metal Roofing Options

We offer a variety of metal roofing styles to meet your specific needs

Standing Seam Metal Roofing

Standing seam metal roofing features vertical panels with concealed fasteners, creating a clean, modern appearance. This premium option offers exceptional durability and weather resistance with minimal maintenance.

  • Contemporary aesthetic
  • Hidden fastener system
  • Superior weather-tight seams

Corrugated Metal Roofing

Corrugated metal roofing is a more economical option featuring a wavy or ribbed pattern. It's lightweight, easy to install, and provides excellent drainage while maintaining durability against the elements.

  • Cost-effective solution
  • Lightweight material
  • Traditional industrial look

Metal Shingles

Metal shingles mimic the appearance of traditional roofing materials like asphalt shingles, slate, or clay tiles, while providing the durability and lifespan of metal. These are perfect for homeowners who want metal's benefits with a classic look.

  • Traditional appearance
  • Metal durability with classic style
  • Various texture and color options

Stone-Coated Steel Roofing

Stone-coated steel roofing combines the strength of steel with the beauty of stone granules. This premium option offers exceptional protection and aesthetic appeal, resembling shake, slate, or tile while being much lighter and more durable.

  • Premium aesthetic appeal
  • Multi-layer protection
  • Enhanced sound insulation

Benefits of Metal Roofing

Why metal roofing is an excellent investment for your property

Longevity

Metal roofs can last 40-70 years with minimal maintenance, compared to 15-30 years for traditional asphalt shingles.

Weather Resistance

Metal roofing can withstand extreme weather conditions including high winds (up to 140 mph), heavy snow, hail, and rain.

Energy Efficiency

Metal roofs reflect solar heat, which can reduce cooling costs by 10-25% during hot summer months.

Eco-Friendly

Most metal roofing materials contain recycled content and are 100% recyclable at the end of their long lifespan.

Fire Resistance

Metal roofs have a Class A fire rating (the highest possible), providing important protection in wildfire-prone areas.

Design Versatility

Available in a wide range of colors, styles, and finishes to complement any architectural style.

Our Process

How we install your new metal roof

1

Initial Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a thorough inspection of your current roof and property to determine the best metal roofing solution. We'll discuss your aesthetic preferences, budget, and specific needs for your home or building.

2

Material Selection & Proposal

Based on your consultation, we'll recommend the best metal roofing options and provide a detailed proposal outlining materials, timeline, cost, and warranty information.

3

Preparation & Removal

If needed, we'll remove your existing roof and inspect the roof deck for any damage that requires repair. Proper preparation ensures a solid foundation for your new metal roof.

4

Installation of Underlayment & Flashing

We install high-quality underlayment, proper flashing, and ventilation systems to create a weather-tight barrier and ensure optimal performance of your metal roof.

5

Metal Roof Installation

Our experienced team carefully installs your chosen metal roofing system according to manufacturer specifications and industry best practices, with meticulous attention to detail.

6

Final Inspection & Cleanup

Upon completion, we perform a thorough inspection to ensure everything meets our high standards, followed by a complete cleanup of your property, leaving it as clean as we found it.
