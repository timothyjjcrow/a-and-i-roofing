Community Involvement

A&I Roofing is proud to be an active member of the Humboldt County community

Our Commitment

At A&I Roofing, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us for over three decades. Our commitment extends beyond providing quality roofing services â€“ we are dedicated to making a positive impact in Humboldt County and surrounding areas.

Throughout the years, we have participated in numerous community initiatives, donated materials and services to those in need, and collaborated with local organizations to enhance the quality of life for residents in our community.

Community Projects

Here are some of the community projects and initiatives we've been proud to be a part of

Habitat for Humanity Partnership

A&I Roofing is a proud partner of Habitat for Humanity, donating materials and labor for several local housing projects. Our team has volunteered on multiple build days, helping to provide safe, affordable housing for families in need.

  • Ongoing since 2010
  • Humboldt County

Roofs for Veterans Program

Through our Roofs for Veterans program, we provide free roof repairs and replacements to veterans in need. Each year, we select several veterans in our community who require roofing assistance but face financial challenges.

  • Annual program since 2015
  • Humboldt and Del Norte Counties

Local School Donations

A&I Roofing regularly contributes to local schools through material donations, educational presentations about the roofing industry, and scholarship opportunities for students interested in pursuing careers in construction trades.

  • Ongoing throughout the year
  • Arcata and surrounding areas

Disaster Relief Support

When natural disasters strike our region, A&I Roofing is quick to respond. We've provided emergency roofing services, donated materials, and offered discounted repairs to help community members rebuild after fires, floods, and storms.

  • As needed during emergencies
  • Throughout Northern California
