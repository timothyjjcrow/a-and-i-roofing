Wood Roofing

Natural beauty and timeless appeal with wood shakes and shingles

Wood Shakes & Shingles

Wood roofing, including cedar shakes and shingles, offers a distinctive, natural beauty that enhances the curb appeal of any home. Known for its rustic charm and durability, wood roofing provides excellent insulation and can withstand harsh weather conditions when properly maintained.

At A&I Roofing, we are experienced in the installation, repair, and maintenance of wood roofing systems. We source high-quality wood products and use proven techniques to ensure your wood roof is not only beautiful but also long-lasting.

Excellent insulation properties
Durable and impact-resistant
Eco-friendly and sustainable material

Wood Roofing Options

Explore the types of wood roofing solutions we provide

Cedar Shakes

Cedar shakes are hand-split or machine-sawn from cedar logs, offering a rustic, textured appearance. They are thicker than shingles and provide excellent durability and insulation. Available in various grades and thicknesses.

  • Rich, textured look
  • High dimensional stability
  • Naturally resistant to decay and insects

Cedar Shingles

Cedar shingles are sawn on both sides for a more uniform, tailored appearance compared to shakes. They are thinner and offer a smoother, more refined look while still providing the natural beauty and benefits of cedar.

  • Smooth, uniform appearance
  • Versatile for various architectural styles
  • Good durability and weather resistance

Pressure-Treated Wood Roofing

Wood shakes and shingles can be pressure-treated with preservatives to enhance their resistance to rot, fungal decay, and insects. This treatment can extend the lifespan of the roof, especially in moist climates.

  • Enhanced decay resistance
  • Increased lifespan
  • Protection against insects

Fire-Retardant Treated Wood

For areas with wildfire concerns, wood roofing can be treated with fire retardants to achieve a Class A, B, or C fire rating. This provides an important safety feature while maintaining the wood's natural beauty.

  • Improved fire safety
  • Meets building code requirements
  • Peace of mind

Our Wood Roofing Installation Process

From consultation to the final nail, quality at every step

1

Consultation & Material Selection

We discuss your vision, assess your property, and help you select the right type of wood (cedar, pine, etc.), style (shakes or shingles), and any necessary treatments (pressure-treated, fire-retardant).

2

Roof Preparation & Old Roof Removal

Existing roofing material is removed, and the roof deck is inspected for any damage. We ensure a clean, solid, and properly ventilated surface for the new wood roof.

3

Installation of Underlayment & Interleaving Felt

A high-quality underlayment is installed over the roof deck. For shakes, an interleaving felt is typically installed between courses to enhance weather resistance and allow the wood to breathe.

4

Wood Shake/Shingle Installation

Our skilled craftsmen meticulously install each wood shake or shingle, ensuring proper alignment, exposure, and fastening according to industry best practices and local building codes.

5

Flashing & Detail Work

Custom flashing is installed around chimneys, valleys, vents, and other roof penetrations to ensure a watertight seal and prevent leaks.

6

Cleanup & Final Inspection

We perform a thorough cleanup of the job site, removing all debris. A final inspection ensures the roof meets our high standards and your complete satisfaction.
