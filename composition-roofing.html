Composition Roofing

Composition roofing is one of the most popular roofing materials for homes across Humboldt County and the United States. Made from a mixture of asphalt, fiberglass, and mineral granules, these shingles offer an excellent balance of affordability, durability, and aesthetic appeal.

At A&I Roofing, we specialize in the installation, repair, and replacement of composition roofs, using only the highest quality materials from trusted manufacturers. With proper installation and maintenance, a composition roof can protect your home for 20-30 years.

Composition Roofing Options

We offer a variety of composition roofing solutions to meet your needs

3-Tab Shingles

3-tab shingles are a traditional and economical option featuring a flat appearance with cutouts (tabs) along their lower edge. They offer good performance at an affordable price point.

  • Budget-friendly
  • 20-25 year lifespan
  • Lightweight design

Architectural Shingles

Architectural (or dimensional) shingles provide a more sophisticated, textured appearance with varied thickness and a multi-dimensional look. These premium shingles offer enhanced durability and visual appeal.

  • Enhanced curb appeal
  • 25-30 year lifespan
  • Superior wind resistance

Luxury Shingles

Premium luxury shingles are designed to replicate the look of natural slate or wood shake roofing. They offer the highest level of quality, durability, and aesthetic appeal among composition options.

  • Premium appearance
  • 30+ year lifespan
  • Maximum weather protection

Cool Roof Shingles

Energy-efficient cool roof shingles feature reflective granules that deflect sunlight and heat away from your home. These specialized shingles can help reduce energy costs and are environmentally friendly.

  • Energy savings
  • Reduced carbon footprint
  • Heat-reflective technology

Our Process

How we install your new composition roof

1

Initial Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a thorough inspection of your current roof and discuss your needs, preferences, and budget constraints. Our experts will recommend the best composition roofing options for your specific situation.

2

Detailed Proposal

You'll receive a comprehensive proposal outlining the scope of work, materials to be used, project timeline, and cost. We'll answer any questions you have to ensure you feel confident in your decision.

3

Roof Preparation

Our team will prepare your roof by removing old shingles and inspecting the decking for any damage that needs repair. We'll also ensure proper ventilation and install ice and water shields where necessary.

4

Material Delivery & Installation

We deliver and install high-quality underlayment, drip edge, flashing, and composition shingles according to manufacturer specifications and industry best practices. Our experienced team works efficiently and with attention to detail.

5

Thorough Cleanup

After installation, we conduct a thorough cleanup of your property, removing all roofing debris and using magnetic sweepers to collect nails and other metal objects to ensure your yard is safe.

6

Final Inspection & Warranty

We conduct a final inspection to ensure everything is installed properly and to your satisfaction. We'll provide you with warranty information and maintenance recommendations to help you get the most out of your new roof.
